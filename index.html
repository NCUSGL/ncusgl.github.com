Nashville Developers!

When

December 10, 2014
6:00 pm - 9:00 pm

Where

Come out and join hundreds of your fellow developers and friends as we celebrate another fantastic year of communal growth with food and drinks.

The Nashville developer community is doing some amazing things. Over the past few years, we've started and grown over 30 active user groups with thousands of members. We have setup three major conferences bringing out thousands more, both local and nationally. Finally, we've created a hackathon that is dominating the Southeast and is a model for bringing people together to make awesome things for both play and work.

Because of all this, we're excited to invite you all out to join us as we celebrate another year of awesome during our 3rd annual Joint User Group Holiday party. This party is for anyone who slings code, especially those of us who partake regularly in one or more of the monthly user groups.
